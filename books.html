CHILDREN

This engaging book celebrates a year of holidays and traditions representing Kenya's many cultural groups. Readers will learn about the strong tradition of song and dance in Kenya and also how family occasions are celebrated in this African country.

On market day, Mama Panya's son Adika invites everyone they see to pancake dinner! How will Mama Panya ever feed them all? With informative endnotes, including a delicious spicy pancake recipe, this clever and heartwarming story about Kenyan village will teach children the importance of sharing even when you have little to give.

When five-year-old Sulwe's classmates make fun of her dark skin, she tries lightening herself to no avail, but her encounter with a shooting star helps her understand there is beauty in every shade.
